ABOUT AMEYA

WHO WE ARE

PT. Creo Ameya Boga (Ameya Boga) is one of the leading logistic companies focusing on kitchen operator and food & beverages support service company, head quarter in Jakarta.

‘Ameya’ stands for boundless, dynamic and prosper, we are obliged to step up to the plate to offer the strategic solutions to running the kitchen and F&B services, an building the profitable business.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

The restaurant industry is a vibrant, creative, fast-paced place to build a business. It's also very difficult to operate the kitchen as owners need to carefully consider all of the variables from legal permits, kitchen equipment, developing menu, find the right staff, developing menu and everything in between to successfully open a restaurant.

with Ameya, we help to manage and navigate those steps and process with our expertise to JUSTIFY COST & VALUE, PROFESSIONALISM & CONSISTENCY, and keep on INNOVATING & be CREATIVE.
